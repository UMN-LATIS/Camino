Navigation

After looking around the plaza and exploring the maps, head across the plaza and follow the street just left of Chuncho (Chaupi Calle) and walk until you come across large stones crossing the street. This is the start of the Inca town.

Notice how the style of the walls changes as soon as you cross this threshold (Something here about polygonal walls and the stone masonry style of these walls?). You’re stepping back to 1520 (right before the Spaniards entered). Take your first left, onto Calle 1, and walk until you emerge on Patacalle, overlooking the Temple hill.
